# Wireless Product Manufacturers Research

## Introduction

In today’s competitive job market, wireless technicians and engineers must know the major products and who manufactures them. This document provides key wireless products, identifies a manufacturer for each, links to the manufacturer product page, and gives a brief description of the product purpose and features.

---

## 1. LoRaWAN Gateway

**Manufacturer:** RAKwireless  
**Link:** https://www.rakwireless.com/en-us/technology/lorawan?utm_source=chatgpt.com

A LoRaWAN gateway receives LoRa radio packets from multiple sensor nodes and forwards them to a network server for processing in large-scale IoT deployments. RAKwireless gateways support multi-channel communication, long-range connectivity, and flexible backhaul options such as Ethernet, Wi-Fi, and cellular.

## 2. Zigbee Module

**Manufacturer:** Nordic Semiconductor  
**Link:** https://www.nordicsemi.com/Products/Low-power-short-range-wireless

Nordic Semiconductor produces low-power wireless modules that support Zigbee as well as other IEEE 802.15.4-based standards for mesh networking in smart home and industrial applications. Their modules are designed for ultra-low power consumption and broad protocol support, enabling robust communication and interoperability.

## 3. Wi-Fi 6 Chipset

**Manufacturer:** Nordic Semiconductor  
**Link:** https://www.nordicsemi.com/Products/Low-power-short-range-wireless

Nordic provides wireless SoCs that integrate Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) capabilities for high-speed data exchange and enhanced network performance in IoT and embedded systems. These chipsets are optimized for power efficiency and multi-protocol support, enabling seamless integration with Bluetooth and other wireless standards.

## 4. NB-IoT Module

**Manufacturer:** Nordic Semiconductor  
**Link:** https://www.nordicsemi.com/Products/Low-power-short-range-wireless

Nordic’s NB-IoT capable modules connect IoT devices to licensed cellular networks with low power consumption and improved coverage, ideal for smart metering and remote monitoring. They support extended battery life and secure data transmission over 3GPP licensed bands.

## 5. Sigfox Device

**Manufacturer:** iFrogLab  
**Link:** https://www.ifroglab.com/?utm_source=chatgpt.com

iFrogLab designs and manufactures Sigfox-compatible modules that enable ultra-low-power communication over the Sigfox LPWAN network for lightweight IoT devices. These devices are typically used for long battery life applications such as asset tracking and environmental sensing due to minimal data transfer requirements.

## 6. Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) Beacon

**Manufacturer:** Minew  
**Link:** https://www.minew.com/product-category/lot-module/?utm_source=chatgpt.com

Minew Bluetooth Low Energy beacons broadcast short, periodic signals that nearby devices can detect, enabling proximity-based applications like indoor positioning and asset tracking. These beacons are designed for long battery life, easy deployment, and compatibility with BLE ecosystems.

---

## 7. Ultra-Wideband (UWB) Transceiver

**Manufacturer:** Nordic Semiconductor  
**Link:** https://www.nordicsemi.com/Products/Low-power-short-range-wireless

UWB transceivers provide precise ranging and positioning capabilities by transmitting ultra-short radio pulses with high accuracy and low interference. Nordic’s UWB solutions are tailored for indoor navigation, secure access control, and spatial awareness in multi-device systems.

## 8. Wi-Fi 6E Access Point

**Manufacturer:** Nordic Semiconductor  
**Link:** https://www.nordicsemi.com/Products/Low-power-short-range-wireless

A Wi-Fi 6E access point provides high-speed wireless connectivity by operating in the 6 GHz spectrum to reduce congestion and increase throughput. Nordic’s Wi-Fi 6E-capable modules support advanced features like OFDMA and MU-MIMO, improving performance in dense network environments.
